Account Deletion & Data Removal

How to remove your data from TradeLab.

No user accounts

TradeLab does not use user accounts or logins. We do not maintain a central profile or database of your personal information.

Because of this, there is no online account to delete. All data used by TradeLab is either:

  • Generated temporarily in memory for simulations, or
  • Stored locally on your device as app data (for example, preferences or recent configurations).

How to delete your data

To remove TradeLab and any associated local data from your device:

  1. Open your device's app settings or app management screen.
  2. Locate TradeLab in the list of installed apps.
  3. Choose Uninstall (and, if offered, confirm that you also want to remove app data).

On most devices, uninstalling the app removes all local data stored by TradeLab. Exact behavior may depend on your operating system and its backup/settings features.

Questions or issues

If you have questions about how TradeLab handles data, or if your app store requires you to submit an account or data deletion request, you can contact us directly.

Contact email: privacy@journalforex.app
